# NOVA Project Dossier

## 1. What is NOVA?
NOVA is your personal Autonomous Productivity Operator—a highly advanced, always-on AI assistant designed to help you manage your digital life, handle tasks, and keep your productivity on track. Think of it as a smart, secure, and proactive digital partner that works tirelessly in the background so you can focus on what matters most.

## 2. Key Capabilities

### 🧠 The Brain (Smart Planning)
When you ask NOVA to do something, it doesn't just guess. It carefully breaks down your request into a logical, step-by-step plan. If it makes a mistake or gets confused, it realizes it and corrects itself automatically before taking any action.

### 🛡️ The Skeleton (Safety & Control)
NOVA operates under strict rules. It only does what it is explicitly allowed to do. It double-checks every action to make sure it's safe and won't accidentally delete or break anything. It even filters out gibberish or accidental commands instantly.

### 🤖 The Nervous System (Always On, Quietly)
NOVA is always ready but never demanding. It quietly checks in the background for new tasks, emails, or files every few minutes without draining your computer's battery or slowing it down. It organizes everything so it's ready when you need it.

### 🎙️ The Voice (Natural Conversation)
You can talk to NOVA naturally. It listens and understands you without you having to pause awkwardly or speak like a robot. Even if you ask something unexpected, NOVA is smart enough to figure out what you mean.

### 💾 The Memory (Remembering What Matters)
NOVA has a powerful memory. It remembers past interactions, files, and important details. When you ask it a question, it quickly searches its memory to give you the most accurate and helpful answer, filtering out duplicates so you only get the best information.

## 3. How It Keeps You Safe

- **Biometric Security:** For any major or high-risk actions, NOVA will ask for your fingerprint (TouchID on your Mac) to make sure it's really you authorizing the action.
- **Action Limits:** It has built-in limits on how many things it can create or update in a day, preventing any out-of-control automated actions.

## 4. Your Dashboard

NOVA comes with a clean, easy-to-use dashboard where you can see everything it's doing:
- **Headquarters (HQ):** A real-time overview of your tasks and your overall "System Health" score—a simple grade on how well you're keeping up with your goals and tasks.
- **Communications:** A quick summary of your most important emails and messages.
- **Security:** A clear view of your system's safety and recent fingerprint check-ins.
- **Reasoning:** A transparent look into how NOVA is thinking and planning your tasks, so you're never in the dark.
- **Monitor:** A simple check on how your computer's resources are holding up.

---
**Summary:** NOVA is built to be your ultimate digital ally: smart, safe, and always working to make your life easier.
